Destinations

Surfy sunrises, heritage lanes, and misty highlands — a few favorites from Sri Lanka.

Sri Lanka · East Coast

Arugambay, Sri Lanka

Beaches • Surf • Sunrise

A laid-back surf town with golden beaches and a vibrant sunrise scene. Peak season brings world-class breaks and easygoing beach cafes.

Highlights & Activities

  • Catch waves at Main Point or Whiskey Point
  • Sunrise hike to Elephant Rock viewpoint
  • Pottuvil Lagoon safari (birds and crocodiles)
  • Fresh seafood and roti at beachfront cafes
  • Sunset yoga or chill on the sand
Sri Lanka · South Coast

Galle, Sri Lanka

UNESCO Fort • Heritage • Coastal City

A charming coastal city centered around the Dutch-built Galle Fort. Cobblestone lanes, sea-battered ramparts, lighthouses, and cozy cafes make it timeless.

Highlights & Activities

  • Golden-hour walk along the Fort ramparts
  • Visit Galle Lighthouse and Meeran Mosque
  • Explore museums and Dutch Reformed Church
  • Gelato and kottu along Pedlar Street
  • Day trip to Unawatuna or Jungle Beach
Sri Lanka · Highlands

Chariot Path, Sri Lanka

Highlands • Hike • Cloud Forest

A serene highland meadow near Nuwara Eliya with sweeping views and Ramayana folklore. Best explored early morning when the mist lifts.

Highlights & Activities

  • Forest trail leading to open grasslands
  • Sunrise panoramas on clear days
  • Wildflowers, birdlife, and cool mountain air
  • Quiet picnic spots (leave no trace)
  • Layer up — temperatures can be crisp
